Why Sindiswa Shop exists

Built by someone who was tired of ordering hair site-unseen.

Sindiswa Shop started with one frustration: buying wigs online in South Africa either meant paying import prices for the same hair sold cheaper abroad, or trusting a local seller's word on "virgin" and "Remy" with no way to check.

We check the hair ourselves before it ever leaves our warehouse, we price for the South African market specifically, and we stock the textures our own customers actually wear — kinky curly, Burmese curly, kinky straight, body wave.
